Acknowledgements

The digitization of the Speculum Romanae Magnificentiae was made possible by a grant from the University of Chicago Provost's Program for Academic Technology Innovation. This ATI grant supported the digitization of the original prints and building of the web site.


Support for the creation of Virtual Itineraries by invited scholars was provided by the Graham Foundation for Advanced Studies in the Fine Arts.


Work on two related projects, an exhibtion of the prints at the University of Chicago Library's Special Collections Research Center and its accompanying catalog, has provided additional information about individual prints which has been added to the metadata made available on this web site. We would like to acknowledge the support of the Special Collections Research Center and the University of Chicago Humanities Division for the work on the exhibition, and the support of the Delmas Foundation, the University of Chicago Library Society, and the Graham Foundation for Advanced Studies in the Fine Arts for the accompanying catalog.
